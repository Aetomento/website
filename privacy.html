What data we handle and why?

This is a static website and therefore does not collect any data by itself. Your IP address may be used to ensure proper communication between services. We do not have insight to your IP address, as this is entirely handled by the servers.

Furthermore, we make use of third party services for statistical purposes and security:


  • Google Analytics for detailed statistics regarding our visitors. Your activities are pseudonymized and stored on Google Inc. or Google Ireland Limited servers with anonymized IP address.

  • Cloudflare Inc for security and protection of our website against DdoS attacks and otherwise malicious actions. All data sent to our website is handled and forwarded by Cloudflare.

Who can access this data and why?

All data described above can only be accessed by the representative of this website and/or any entity that may own this website and/or the virtual servers that this website may be hosted on. The third party services generally only collect the data, and do not themselves surrender any insight to their employees.

Your lawful rights

While you have your lawful right to information and correction of personal data, we cannot comply with requests for this as we do not handle any data ourselves. Similarly, third party services do not provide any unique identifiers to tie data to users.


Furthermore, as per GDPR §77, you have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority if you conclude that the processing of your personal data does not go accordingly to the GDPR and may therefore be illicit.


Failure to agree to this policy means we can not offer our services to you as the data collected is data required for optimal functionality
